Our Mission

The St. James Foundation (TSJF) exists to serve James Island and the surrounding communities of Charleston and the South Carolina Lowcountry as a public nonprofit corporation. TSJF addresses the needs of individuals and families by establishing programs and engaging in collaborative ventures with other organizations to reduce the effects of poverty, inadequate education, substance abuse, violence, unemployment and other social problems that reach across the spectrum of age, gender, or religion for the benefit of the community as a whole.
