Young Entrepreneurial Associates

Starting in August 2005, Rev. Charles Hayward started this program to begin investments in young futures.

Purpose: to expose and educate students about business, corporate leadership, entrepreneurship, personal and family financial matters.

  • The target population are 12 students per year from the 6th, 7th and 8th grades.
  • The program has served 445 students, some students participating in all three grades.
  • In 2021 the YBA program was revised to reach those having started a new business and want to advance to the next level of productivity, or those who have an idea and vision to start a new business in the immediate future.
